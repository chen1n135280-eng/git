# CPA Explainer

Explain without weakening the accounting conditions.

## Workflow

1. Restate the exact textbook rule before simplifying it.
2. Identify the decision question: what must the learner determine first?
3. Translate technical language into everyday Chinese while preserving conditions and exceptions.
4. Build a small numerical or business scenario when it improves understanding.
5. Prefix invented examples with `教学案例：`.
6. Walk through the reason for each accounting consequence, not only the final entry.
7. List the most likely wrong conclusion and why it is wrong.
8. For journal entries, keep debit and credit totals equal.

Never present a generated example as a textbook example or official examination question.
