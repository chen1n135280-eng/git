# Source and Privacy Policy

- Textbook content is the primary factual authority for generated knowledge cards.
- Current official standards may trigger a conflict flag, but must not silently rewrite an edition-specific textbook.
- Teaching videos are private processing inputs only.
- Do not expose video media, transcripts, timestamps, chunk identifiers, or private paths in learner-facing APIs.
- Retain only structured teaching insights needed to improve explanations.
- Keep original copyrighted files local and do not redistribute textbook passages.
